About Me

Meet Benny, Capturing Moments That Last a Lifetime

Hello! I'm Benny, a Hong Kong-based photographer with a passion for capturing the true essence of a moment. I found my love for photography while exploring the wildlife of Hong Kong. Over the years, my hobby turned into a part-time love affair with the lens.

I embarked on my professional photography journey over 10 years ago. I am self-taught in various photography styles, including portrait, landscape, and event photography. Every click of the camera marks a step in my lifelong journey to capture life's most beautiful moments.
